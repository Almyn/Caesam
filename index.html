Campus App for Ecofriendly Shopping on an Adapted Marketplace


Give us you opinion !

While garment manufacturing accounts for 20% of global industrial water pollution, nearly 60% of all clothing produced ends up in incinerators or landfills within a year of being made. No more statistics are needed to show the extend of the damage the fashion industry is causing to our only planet. Given the urge to act concretely, we want to create a marketplace app on which students of the campus could buy and sell clothing to other students of the campus, allowing them to consume locally second hand.Yet we won't stop at clothing : the recurrent posts on Facebook of people trying to sell their fridge or rent a waffle machine show there is a real need of a proper marketplace in this field ... that our app can fulfill.
